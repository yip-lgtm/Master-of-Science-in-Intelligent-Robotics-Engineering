## 🏪 Where to Buy (by category)

### 1. Silicone (Ecoflex 00-30) — **CRITICAL**

**Option A: Smooth-On HK Distributor (Recommended)**
- **Polytek Development Corp HK** (or similar)
- Email: sales@polytek.com (US-based but ships to HK)
- Price: ~HK$180-220 per 1-lb kit (Part A + B)
- Lead time: 3-5 days (delivery from US) or 1-2 days if local stock
- Note: Search "Smooth-On Hong Kong distributor" for current local agent

**Option B: Local Alternative (Faster, Cheaper)**
- **Dragon Skin 10** (Smooth-On equivalent, same properties)
- Available at: Apliu Street (鴨脷洲) hobby shops
- Price: ~HK$150-180 per 1-lb kit
- Pros: Same 1:1 mixing ratio, similar cure time

**Option C: AliExpress (Cheapest, Slowest)**
- Search: "Ecoflex 00-30 silicone"
- Price: ~HK$120-150 (with 2-3 week shipping)
- Risk: Quality varies, may have batch inconsistency

**Recommendation**: Try Dragon Skin 10 at Apliu Street first (fastest, similar quality).

### 2. Electronics (Arduino, Sensors, Valves)

**Golden Computer Centre (高登電腦中心)** — Sham Shui Po
- ✅ Arduino Uno: ~HK$80-100
- ✅ Breadboard, jumper wires: ~HK$30-50
- ✅ LEDs, resistors, buttons: ~HK$20-30
- ⚠️ Limited sensor/valve selection

**Apliu Street (鴨脷洲街) Flea Market** — Sham Shui Po
- ✅ Cheap electronic components
- ✅ Some sensors (pressure, force)
- ⚠️ Quality varies, no warranty
- Best for: budget prototyping

**TaoBao / AliExpress** (Online)
- ✅ Solenoid valves 12V: ~HK$80-150 each
- ✅ Pressure sensor 0-100kPa: ~HK$100-150
- ✅ FSR402 force sensor: ~HK$60-100
- ✅ MOSFETs (IRF540N): ~HK$10 for 10pcs
- Lead time: 7-14 days

**RS Components (rs-online.com)**
- ✅ Industrial-grade components
- ✅ Same-day delivery in HK
- ⚠️ Higher price but reliable
- Best for: production-quality parts

**Recommended order**:
1. Arduino + breadboard + LEDs from Golden Computer Centre (same day)
2. Valves + sensors from AliExpress (1-2 weeks)
3. Solenoid valves: try RS Components for faster delivery

**Silicone Tubing (4mm ID, 6mm OD)**:
- **McMaster-Carr** (US, ships to HK): ~HK$50-80 for 2m
- **AliExpress**: ~HK$25-40 for 5m
- **Local hardware stores**: ~HK$30-50 for 2m

## ⚠️ Tips for HK Sourcing

1. **Golden Computer Centre closes around 7-8 PM** — plan visits during day
2. **Apliu Street** is best on weekends (more vendors, lower prices)
3. **Cash is king** at Apliu Street (some vendors don't take Octopus/EPS)
4. **AliExpress** for unusual parts (sensors, valves) — expect 1-2 weeks
5. **RS Components** for fast delivery (1-2 days in HK) but higher price
6. **HKUST / PolyU / CityU 3D print services** if you have student access (cheapest)
7. **Bring your own USB cable + laptop** to Golden Computer for Arduino testing
