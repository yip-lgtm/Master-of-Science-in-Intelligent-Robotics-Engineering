# CUHK BEng Mechanical and Automation Engineering (MAE)

> **Programme:** BEng Mechanical and Automation Engineering
> **University:** The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK)
> **Source:** https://www4.mae.cuhk.edu.hk/mechanical-and-automation-engineering/
> **Status:** Self-Study Reference (not enrolled, but relevant for background)

## Programme Description

The Mechanical and Automation Engineering Programme emphasizes the impact of
modern automation technologies on current and future developments in the field
of mechanical engineering. The Programme stresses a balanced curriculum in both
basic theory and hands-on practice, and covering subject areas as mechanics
and materials, thermodynamics, mechanical design, manufacturing processes,
mechatronics and robotics.

## Streams (Major Elective Specializations)

1. **Design and Manufacturing** — CAD/CAM, FEA, VR, Additive Mfg
2. **Robotics and Automation** — Modern Control, Robotics, Medical Robotics
3. **Mechatronics** — Embedded Systems, Sensors, Actuators
4. **Energy** — Thermofluids, Power Systems
5. **Bio-Medical** — Medical Robotics, Biomechanics
6. **Materials** — Nanomaterials, Composites

## Relationship to IRE Bootcamp

This MAE programme provides **undergraduate-level depth** in:
- Mechanics & Materials (Week 8 of IRE)
- Thermodynamics & Heat Transfer
- Mechanical Design (relevant to all IRE weeks)
- Manufacturing Technology
- Control Systems (Week 5 of IRE)
- Mechatronics (Week 6 of IRE — direct overlap)

The PolyU MSc IRE Bootcamp builds on **equivalent undergrad** knowledge
that CUHK MAE students would have already mastered.
