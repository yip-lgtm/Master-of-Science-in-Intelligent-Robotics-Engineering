## 4-Year Typical Study Plan
(Refer to official PDF for exact sequencing)

**Year 1**: Faculty Package + Foundation + University Core
**Year 2**: Major Required (Mechanics, Materials, Thermo, Circuits, etc.)
**Year 3**: Advanced Required (Control, Design, Fluid, Manufacturing) + Electives
**Year 4**: FYP + Advanced Electives + Remaining Core
