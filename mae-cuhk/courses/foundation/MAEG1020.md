# MAEG1020 - Computational Design and Fabrication

## 📋 Course Info
- **Code:** MAEG1020
- **Title:** Computational Design and Fabrication
- **Units:** 3
- **Term:** 2 (Year 1)
- **Category:** Foundation

## 🎯 Course Description
Introduction to computational thinking for design and fabrication. Covers
parametric design, digital fabrication techniques, and the use of software
tools for translating design concepts into physical prototypes.

## 📚 Topics (Estimated)
- Parametric design (Grasshopper, Fusion 360)
- 2D/3D modeling fundamentals
- Laser cutting, 3D printing basics
- CNC machining concepts
- Design for Manufacturing (DFM)

## 🔗 Prerequisites
- None (or ENGG1110 co-req)

## 💡 Industry Applications
- Product design
- Architectural design
- Rapid prototyping
- Maker culture

## 📊 Relevance to IRE Bootcamp
- Low (more design-focused, less robotics)
- Useful for designing robot chassis/enclosures
