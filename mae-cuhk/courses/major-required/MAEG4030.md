# MAEG4030 Heat Transfer

**Course Type**: Major Required  
**Units**: 3

## Official Description
This course will cover fundamental topics in heat transfer including physical origins of three heat transfer modes and their rate equations, heat diffusion equation, boundary and initial conditions, steady-state and transient conduction, numerical methods, boundary layer equation, external and internal forced convection, free convection, and thermal radiation.

## Key Topics
- Conduction (steady and transient)
- Convection (forced and free)
- Radiation
- Heat exchangers
- Numerical methods in heat transfer

## Learning Outcomes
- Apply the three modes of heat transfer
- Solve conduction and convection problems
- Analyze heat exchangers and radiation exchange

## Self-Study Notes & Resources
- **Textbook**: Incropera Fundamentals of Heat and Mass Transfer
- **Key Concepts**: Fourier’s law, Newton’s law of cooling, Stefan-Boltzmann law, Nusselt number
- **Relation to Robotics / My Projects**:
  - Important for thermal management of motors, electronics, and actuators in robots
  - Relevant for designing safe and efficient robotic systems

## Practical Application in My Simulator
- Considered thermal limits when selecting actuators for physical robot versions

## Practice & Exercises
- Solve 1D and 2D conduction problems
- Convection coefficient calculations
- Heat exchanger effectiveness
