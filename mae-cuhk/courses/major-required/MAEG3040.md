# MAEG3040 Mechanical Design

**Course Type**: Major Required  
**Units**: 3

## Official Description
This course covers the engineering design principles, machine design methods, design of machine components, and kinematics. Load analysis, design for manufacturing, reliability and human factors will also be introduced and reviewed.

## Key Topics
- Engineering design process
- Machine elements design (shafts, bearings, gears, etc.)
- Kinematics of mechanisms
- Load analysis and stress considerations
- Design for manufacturing and reliability

## Learning Outcomes
- Apply systematic design process
- Design basic machine components
- Perform load and stress analysis in design

## Self-Study Notes & Resources
- **Textbook**: Shigley’s Mechanical Engineering Design
- **Relation to Robotics / My Projects**:
  - Directly relevant to designing robot links, joints, and grippers
  - Supports mechanical structure design in my 3R arm projects

## Practical Application in My Simulator
- Used design principles when conceptualizing the mechanical structure of the simulated arm and potential physical prototypes

## Practice & Exercises
- Design simple shafts and couplings
- Kinematic analysis of mechanisms
- Failure mode and reliability considerations
