**Course Type**: Research Component

## Official Description
The course is designed to provide students with an opportunity to carry out, under the supervision of an academic staff, an independent project with research elements in engineering.

## Key Topics
- Literature review and problem definition
- Project planning and methodology
- Literature survey and background study
- Preliminary design / experimentation / simulation
- Progress reporting and presentation

## Learning Outcomes
- Conduct independent research/project work
- Apply engineering knowledge to solve real problems
- Communicate technical ideas effectively
