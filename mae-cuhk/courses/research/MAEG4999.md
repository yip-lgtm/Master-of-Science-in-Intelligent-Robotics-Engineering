# MAEG4999 Final Year Project II

**Course Type**: Research Component  
**Units**: 3

## Official Description
The course is designed to provide students with an opportunity to carry out, under the supervision of an academic staff, an independent project with research elements in engineering. (Continuation of MAEG4998)

## Key Topics
- Detailed design / implementation / experimentation
- Data analysis and results discussion
- Final report writing and presentation / demonstration
- Reflection on engineering practice and learning outcomes

## Learning Outcomes
- Complete an independent engineering project
- Analyze results and draw conclusions
- Produce professional technical report and presentation

## Self-Study Notes & Resources
- **Relation to Robotics / My Projects**:
  - Continuation of ideas from MAEG4998 — e.g. full development of advanced robotic arm with mechatronics integration, or intelligent warehouse system with Agent/LLM control

- Analyze performance (accuracy, speed, success rate of pick-and-place)
- Write final report and prepare demo
