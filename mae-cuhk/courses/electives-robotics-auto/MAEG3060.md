# MAEG3060 - Introduction to Robotics

## 📋 Course Info
- **Code:** MAEG3060
- **Title:** Introduction to Robotics
- **Units:** 3
- **Term:** Elective (Design/Robotics/Mechatronics streams)
- **Category:** Major Elective (Robotics and Automation)

## 🎯 Course Description
Introduction to robotic systems, covering kinematics, dynamics, control,
sensors, and end effectors. Hands-on with robot manipulators and mobile
robots. (Directly relevant to IRE Bootcamp content!)

## 📚 Topics
- Robot classifications and applications
- Coordinate transformations and homogeneous transforms
- Forward kinematics (DH parameters)
- Inverse kinematics (geometric and analytical)
- Jacobians and velocity kinematics
- Trajectory planning
- Robot control (joint space, task space)
- Sensors and actuators
- End effectors and grippers
- Mobile robots (kinematics, localization)

## 🔗 Prerequisites
- MAEG2020 Engineering Mechanics
- MAEG3050 Introduction to Control Systems (recommended)
- Linear algebra

## 💡 Industry Applications
- Industrial automation
- Service robotics
- Medical robotics
- Autonomous vehicles
