# ENGG5404 Micromachining and Microelectromechanical Systems (MEMS)

**Course Type**: Major Elective
**Units**: 3
**Stream**: Design and Manufacturing / Robotics (micro-scale)

## Official Description
Micro-scale fabrication and MEMS devices. Covers silicon micromachining,
lithography, etching, thin-film deposition, and applications of MEMS sensors
and actuators in mechanical systems.

## Key Topics
- MEMS overview and applications
- Silicon crystal structure
- Photolithography
- Wet and dry etching
- Thin-film deposition (PVD, CVD)
- Bulk micromachining
- Surface micromachining
- MEMS sensors (accelerometer, gyroscope, pressure)
- MEMS actuators (comb drive, cantilever)
- Packaging and integration

## Learning Outcomes
- Understand MEMS fabrication processes
- Design simple MEMS devices
- Select appropriate MEMS sensors
- Integrate MEMS into larger systems
