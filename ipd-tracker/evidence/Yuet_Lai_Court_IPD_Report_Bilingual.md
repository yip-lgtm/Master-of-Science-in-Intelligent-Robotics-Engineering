# 1. Introduction / 引言
In the ICE system, IPD is the main work-based stage you need to complete before you can go for Chartered Engineer status. This report pulls together the evidence from the Yuet Lai Court Dangerous Hillside project (DH Order DH0008/NT/21/C) to show how I've developed across the 7 Core Attributes.

## Project Summary / 項目摘要
This was a statutory slope remedial project on a dangerous cut slope (Feature 11NW-A/C16, Sub-division No.1) at Yuet Lai Court. The main works involved replacing the upper 40° slope with 3m of no-fines concrete using a pit-by-pit method, and installing three rows of soil nails at 2.0m spacing on the lower 52° slope. The scope covered everything from the initial investigation and analysis through to design, BD approval, construction supervision and monitoring.
這是一項法定斜坡修復工程，針對悅麗苑危險切坡（特徵編號 11NW-A/C16 第1分區）。主要工程包括上部40°斜坡採用逐坑開挖法置換3米深無細粒混凝土，以及下部52°斜坡安裝三排土壤釘（水平間距2.0米）。範圍涵蓋從初步勘察分析到設計、屋宇署批准、施工監督及監測的全部過程。

Application to Yuet Lai Court Project / 悅麗苑項目應用:
I used SLOPE/W with the Morgenstern-Price method together with PLAXIS 3D to check the existing slope (FOS was between 0.870 and 1.051) and then designed the remedial works to bring the factor of safety above 1.1.
我使用SLOPE/W（Morgenstern-Price方法）結合PLAXIS 3D，檢查現有斜坡穩定性（安全係數介乎0.870至1.051），並設計修復工程使安全係數超過1.1。
For the tight urban site next to Cho Yiu Chuen, I chose a pit-by-pit excavation with staged timber shoring because it gave the best balance between safety, buildability and cost.
對於毗鄰祖堯邨的狹窄市區工地，我選擇逐坑開挖配合分階段 timber shoring，因為這在安全、施工可行性及成本之間取得最佳平衡。
I took the lab test results (c', φ', s'-t' plots) and worked out the design parameters and earth pressure coefficients myself.
我自行分析實驗室測試結果（c'、φ'、s'-t' 圖），計算設計參數及土壓力係數。
I identified the main risks early — settlement to the adjacent buildings, groundwater and the proximity to MTR — and made sure we had proper monitoring in place.
我及早識別主要風險——鄰近樓宇沉降、地下水及港鐵鄰近問題——並確保設置妥善監測。
Step-by-Step Key Calculations & References / 關鍵計算步驟與參考文獻:
#### **Step 1: Existing Slope Stability Check (SLOPE/W)**
I ran the analysis using Morgenstern-Price with the worst groundwater case (water table at one-third of the slope height). The existing factor of safety came out at 0.870–1.051, which is below the GEO requirement of 1.1 for a Dangerous Hillside Order.
Reference: Geoguide 5, GEO TGN 10

#### **Step 2: Designing the Remedial Works**
Upper slope (40°): 3m deep no-fines concrete replacement, excavated pit by pit.
Lower slope (52°): Three rows of soil nails at 2.0m horizontal spacing.
After the remedial works the factor of safety went above 1.1 as required.
Reference: Geoguide 5, CIRIA C580, Geoguide 7

#### **Step 3: Checking Excavation Effects with PLAXIS 3D**
I modelled the staged pit excavation in 3D to see what settlement we would get at the adjacent raft-founded buildings. Maximum settlement came out at only 3.061 mm — well below the 25 mm limit.
Reference: GEO TGN 15, CIRIA C580

#### **Step 4: Soil Nail Design Checks**
I checked pull-out capacity, structural capacity of the nails and the head plate details, then optimised the layout for overall stability.
Reference: Geoguide 7, BS 8006-2
**步驟1：現有斜坡穩定性檢查（SLOPE/W）**
我採用Morgenstern-Price方法，配合最不利地下水情況（水位在斜坡高度三分之一處）進行分析。現有安全係數為0.870–1.051，低於危險斜坡令要求的1.1。
參考：Geoguide 5, GEO TGN 10

**步驟2：修復工程設計**
上部斜坡（40°）：3米深無細粒混凝土逐坑置換。
下部斜坡（52°）：三排土壤釘，水平間距2.0米。
修復後安全係數超過1.1，符合要求。
參考：Geoguide 5, CIRIA C580, Geoguide 7

**步驟3：PLAXIS 3D檢查開挖影響**
我以三維有限元模擬分階段坑道開挖，評估對鄰近筏式基礎樓宇的沉降影響。最大沉降僅為3.061毫米，遠低於25毫米限值。
參考：GEO TGN 15, CIRIA C580

**步驟4：土壤釘設計驗證**
我檢查拉拔承載力、釘身結構承載力及頭板細節，並優化佈置以確保整體穩定性。
參考：Geoguide 7, BS 8006-2
Reflection & Competence Demonstrated / 反思與能力展示:
Working on this live DH Order project gave me the chance to actually use the theory I learned at university on a real job. I got comfortable running both limit-equilibrium and finite-element analyses, making my own engineering judgements on what FOS to target and how to sequence the works, and backing everything up with proper calculations and references. It was good to see the full cycle — from spotting the problem, through design, to checking how it would actually perform on site.
參與這個真實的危險斜坡令項目，讓我有機會將大學所學理論應用於實際工作中。我熟悉了極限平衡法及有限元分析，能獨立判斷目標安全係數及施工順序，並以計算及參考文獻支持所有決定。親身經歷從發現問題、設計到現場驗證的完整過程，感覺非常充實。

I made sure we followed the GEO Code of Practice, the relevant PNAPs and the Buildings Ordinance, and that TCP T3 supervision was in place for all the soil nailing works.

我們設立妥善的監測系統（沉降標、孔隙水壓力計及振動監測器），每兩週向屋宇署及地質工程處提交報告，並設定清晰觸發水平及應變計劃。
For the excavation itself I designed staged timber shoring and made sure no two adjacent pits were open at the same time.
開挖工程方面，我設計分階段 timber shoring，並確保相鄰坑道不同時開挖。
I checked the settlement effect on the neighbouring raft-founded buildings and kept it to a maximum of 3.061 mm, which is well inside the 25 mm limit.
我評估對鄰近筏式基礎樓宇的沉降影響，並將最大沉降控制在3.061毫米，遠低於25毫米限值。
Step-by-Step Key Calculations & References / 關鍵計算步驟與參考文獻:
#### **Settlement Check (Safety Verification)**
I used the influence zone method from TGN 15 to estimate how much the adjacent buildings would settle due to the 3m excavation. The calculated maximum settlement was only 3.061 mm — comfortably below the 25 mm GEO/BD limit. This confirmed there was no real risk of damage to the neighbouring structures.
Reference: GEO TGN 15, Geoguide 5
**沉降檢查（安全驗證）**
我採用TGN 15的影響範圍法，估算3米開挖對鄰近樓宇的沉降影響。計算所得最大沉降僅為3.061毫米，遠低於地質工程處／屋宇署25毫米限值，確認鄰近結構無實際損壞風險。
參考：GEO TGN 15, Geoguide 5
Reflection & Competence Demonstrated / 反思與能力展示:
Safety was always at the front of my mind on this statutory DH Order project. I had to identify the hazards, design safe methods of working, and then actively supervise the works on site. I compared the whole-life performance of the chosen solution against traditional sprayed concrete, looking at carbon, maintenance access and how it would look in the landscape.
我比較所選方案與傳統噴漿混凝土的全生命週期表現，包括碳排放、維修通道及景觀效果。

能夠將可持續發展思維帶入傳統「硬」工程項目中，感覺很有意義。採用透水材料及生物工程方法，讓我們能提供技術上表現良好、同時對環境更友好且長期維修成本更低的方案。
